th3a's personas

To install, add the repository to your controller by issuing 

@fs add https://th3a.faen.dev/personas/
, then execute eg. 
@unownedmare.p
to load the persona

mare.p

mare.p
renames the unit to 
Mare Unit $name
, where 
$name
is the unit's current name.
It also bans the unit from accessing it's own controller. Make sure you have another Owner set!

unownedmare.p

Like 

mare.p
but doesn't ban the unit, useful for self-owned units.
